UPSC

About UPSC

The UPSC conducts various examinations in accordance with the Rules of examination as notified by the Government of India in a just, fair and impartial manner for making a merit based selection and recommendation of candidates for various Group A and Group B Services of the Govt. of India.

SSC

About SSC

SSC is considered a major body of the Department of Personnel and Training, this body is run by a chairman, two members, and a secretary-cum-controller of examinations. SSC also stands for Secondary School Certificate; this exam is conducted by many boards like CBSE and state boards for 10th-grade students.

Bank Exams

About Bank Exams

These are computer-based tests and conducted in different phases to choose the right candidate for various posts such as clerk, Specialist Officers, Probationary Officers, Managers, Senior Managers and so on. A large number of candidates appear for the competitive exams and aspire to join one of the prestigious banks.

Insurance Exams

About Insurance Exams

Insurance Exams in India are conducted for various posts in Insurance sector. IRDA or Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India is responsible for the promotion of jobs in insurance industry in India. The exams are conducted on regular intervals by the conducting bodies as required and the recruitment is concluded.

Defence Exams

About Defence Exams

Defense exams are tests that are conducted to select candidates for various jobs in the Indian defense forces. The defense forces consist of three branches: the Indian Army, the Indian Navy, and the Indian Air Force. These branches protect the country from external and internal threats and also provide disaster relief.

Exams After 12th

About

Candidates after completing 12th can appear for various government exams such as RRB ALP, Stenographer, etc.The common subjects of various government exams are General Awareness, Reasoning Ability, Quantitative Aptitude, and English. A detailed list of subjects along with the topics covered is given in the syllabus page of respective exams.
